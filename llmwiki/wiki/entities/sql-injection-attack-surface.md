Các bề mặt attack surface được nhắc đến:

- classic relational databases
- JSON/JSONB operators
- full-text/search
- geospatial
- window functions
- CTEs
- lateral joins
- ORMs
- query builders
- stored procedures
- search servers
- reporting/exporters
